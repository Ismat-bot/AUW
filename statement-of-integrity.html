Statement of Integrity

Asian University for Women is dedicated to upholding the highest standards of integrity and ethical conduct. As an institution that values transparency, accountability, and mutual respect, we are committed to fostering an environment that encourages honesty and trust among all members of our community.

All students, faculty, staff, and partners of AUW are expected to adhere to these values in their academic, professional, and personal conduct. The University‚Äôs policies are designed to support this commitment and ensure that violations of ethical standards are addressed promptly and fairly.

We believe that integrity is the foundation of academic excellence and social responsibility. As such, we continually strive to promote these principles through our curriculum, institutional policies, and daily interactions on campus.

By choosing to be part of the AUW community, every individual affirms their responsibility to uphold these ideals and contribute positively to a culture of ethical behavior and respect.

Asian University for Women (AUW) adheres to the highest standards of integrity in all its affairs. Still, we recognize, mistakes are made, intentionally or otherwise. Without knowledge of any misdeeds that may have occurred, we are unable to take corrective action. We request the public to notify AUW of any real or suspected misdeed through this anonymous portal. A misdeed is a misdeed ‚Äî it can be financial malfeasance; sexual harassment; policy or administrative dysfunctions.
