AUW SUPPORT FOUNDATIONS

Asian University for Women Support Foundation in Cambridge, Massachusetts, USA is a 501(c)3-registered nonprofit organization (EIN: 36-4483980). Incorporated in 2001, it is governed by a Board of Directors, several of whom also serve on the AUW Board of Trustees. The AUWSF in Cambridge lends counsel and support to other Support Foundations and Groups throughout the world.

For donations in U.S., please contact give(at)asian-university.org. Please mail check donations to: P.O. Box 380768, Cambridge, MA 02238, USA. For stocks and wire donations, please contact give(at)asian-university.org. Online credit card donations are available from this link: https://asian-university.org/donate.
