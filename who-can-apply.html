Who Can Apply

Asian University for Women (AUW) welcomes applications from academically talented women from diverse backgrounds across South Asia, Southeast Asia, and the Middle East. The university seeks students who demonstrate leadership potential, a commitment to social change, and the ability to thrive in a rigorous liberal arts environment.

The following criteria are generally considered for eligibility:

  • Applicants must identify as women and be between 16 and 24 years of age at the time of enrollment.
  • Completed secondary education or equivalent, with strong academic records.
  • Demonstrated leadership qualities and community involvement.
  • English proficiency sufficient to engage in academic work (AUW provides language support).
  • Motivation to contribute to positive social change in their communities and countries.
  • Financial need is considered but not a requirement for admission.

AUW is committed to providing opportunities to women from marginalized and underrepresented communities, including refugees and internally displaced persons. The admissions process also values diversity in ethnicity, nationality, religion, and socioeconomic background.

Interested applicants should review the full admissions requirements and prepare the necessary documents before applying. AUW encourages early application to maximize access to scholarships and support.
