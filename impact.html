Since 2008, we have provided exceptional higher education to almost 2,000 current students and alumnae. At AUW, we empower women not only from refugee camps, but plantations, garment factories, tea estates, conflict zones, and urban slums. What they all have in common is a deep resolve to transform not just their own lives, but their families and communities through a purposeful education for purpose. When education is their right, beyond inclusion comes real impact.
