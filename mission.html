MISSION & VISION

Asian University for Women (AUW) seeks to offer high quality education to young women drawn from diverse national, ethnic and socio-economic backgrounds...

AUW seeks to graduate women who will be skilled and innovative professionals, service-oriented leaders...

  • Provide a vibrant and diverse residential learning community...
