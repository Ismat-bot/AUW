Masters Programs

The Asian University for Women offers master's programs designed to deepen academic knowledge and leadership skills. Our graduate studies focus on interdisciplinary research and social impact in the Asian context.

Available Masters Programs

  • Master of Arts in Economics
  • Master of Arts in Gender and Women's Studies
  • Master of Arts in International Relations
  • Master of Science in Environmental Science and Policy

Program Features

  • Two-year master's degree programs
  • Emphasis on interdisciplinary learning and critical inquiry
  • Experienced faculty and research mentors
  • Engagement with regional and global issues through research and community projects
  • Opportunities for internships and fieldwork
  • Small class sizes fostering personalized learning

Application Process

Prospective students are encouraged to visit the Admissions page for detailed application instructions and deadlines. Our admissions team is available to assist with queries related to program suitability and application requirements.
